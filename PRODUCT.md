# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Wedding guests of Андрій & Єлизавета. Demographics and device split TBD; assume mixed ages and primarily mobile access. Guests arrive via a direct link shared by the couple.

## Product Purpose

A Ukrainian-language digital wedding invitation. Guests should be able to quickly understand event details, confirm attendance via RSVP, and feel the warmth and occasion of the wedding before arriving.

## Brand Personality

TBD per client brief. The couple's dress code (white, gold, light beige, beige, bronze) suggests a warm, refined aesthetic — but visual direction will be confirmed by the client.

## Anti-references

None specified.

## Design Principles

- Guest-first clarity: every section answers a question a guest actually has (when, where, what to wear, how to confirm).
- Emotional presence: the page should feel like receiving an invitation, not reading a form.
- Mobile-ready by default: guests will open this on their phones from a message link.
- Ukrainian throughout: all copy, labels, and UI in Ukrainian with no language fallbacks.
